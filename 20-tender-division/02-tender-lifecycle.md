# Tender Lifecycle — Moroccan Public Procurement Workflow

Operational reference for the division. Legal frame: **Decree n° 2-22-431
(08/03/2023) on public procurement** (in force since 09/2023), CCAG-T for works,
CPS/RC per tender. The PMMP (www.marchespublics.gov.ma, run by the TGR) is the
mandatory electronic channel. This document encodes the workflow; the CPS of
each tender always prevails — Extractor must flag any deviation from defaults.

## 1. Sources & Detection (Sentinel)

| Source | Content | Cadence |
|---|---|---|
| PMMP | Avis AOO/AOR/concours, DCE downloads, modifications, results | 3×/day |
| Programmes prévisionnels | Buyers' annual planned purchases — **forward radar** for pre-positioning (docs, qualifications, partners) | quarterly sweep |
| Buyer portals (ONEE, ORMVAs, ADM, OCP, ONCF, agences urbaines, ABHs) | Establishment-specific tenders & supplier qualification systems | daily |
| Bulletin Officiel / press | Concessions, PPP, special procedures | weekly |

Procedure types handled: appel d'offres ouvert (AOO — default), restreint (AOR),
concours, marchés négociés, consultations for bons de commande, marchés-cadres,
marchés allotis (per-lot strategy), groupements (conjoint/solidaire).

## 2. Standard Timeline (state machine, deadline-anchored)

```
J-21+  detected      Avis published; DCE downloaded; parsed (≤4h)
J-20   qualified     A3 eliminatory check vs company profile
J-18   G1 GO         Direction decides; orchestrator builds back-plan
J-17   mobilized     Caution provisoire requested (bank), visite des lieux
                     scheduled if required, échantillons if required
J-14   costing       B3 baseline from BPU/DQE + price book + supplier quotes
J-10   technical     B2 mémoire technique, planning, moyens — draft 1
J-7    compliance    B1 full dossier assembly; missing-doc escalation
J-3    G2 PRICE      B4 scenarios → Direction picks final offer/rabais
J-2    final audit   B1 conformity re-audit; signatory review
J-1    G3 SUBMIT     Electronic submission on PMMP (personal certificate);
                     receipt archived
J      opening       Séance d'ouverture des plis (public); A1 captures
J+x    evaluation    Commission d'appel d'offres; possible clarifications
J+y    award         Résultats published → won / lost → debrief (mandatory)
```

Per-tender plan is generated by the Orchestrator from actual deadline and CPS
specifics (visite des lieux dates, échantillon deadlines override defaults).

## 3. Dossier Structure (what B1 assembles — per RC, typical AOO travaux)

**Dossier administratif** (vault-sourced, validity-checked):
déclaration sur l'honneur; registre de commerce; attestation fiscale (<1 an);
attestation CNSS (<1 an); certificat de qualification & classification
(secteur/qualification/classe required by RC); caution provisoire (bank);
statuts + pouvoirs du signataire; ICE/IF identifiers; for groupements:
convention de groupement.

**Dossier technique**: références (attestations de bonne exécution), moyens
humains (CVs, diplômes), moyens matériels (cartes grises, attestations de
propriété/leasing).

**Offre technique** (when RC requires): mémoire technique (méthodologie,
organisation chantier, planning, hygiène & sécurité, qualité), sometimes scored
with thresholds — Extractor must capture the grading grid; B2 writes to the grid.

**Offre financière**: acte d'engagement; bordereau des prix – détail estimatif
(BPU-DQE) filled exactly per template; sous-détail des prix if required.
Arithmetic is verified twice (B3 rule-check + independent recomputation) —
arithmetic errors are handled per decree rules but signal sloppiness.

## 4. Financial Mechanics (B4 cash model per tender)

| Instrument | Default rule (CPS prevails) | System behavior |
|---|---|---|
| Caution provisoire | Fixed amount in avis | Bank request workflow; **restitution chase** after award (cash leaks here) |
| Caution définitive | 3% of marché within 30 days of notification | Auto-task on win; bank line monitoring |
| Retenue de garantie | 10% of each décompte, capped at 7% of marché | Modeled in cash curve; release at réception définitive |
| Pénalités de retard | Per CPS/CCAG-T (capped, typically 8–10%) | C3 flags atypical penalty clauses at G1 |
| Délai de garantie | Typically 12 months (réception provisoire → définitive) | Auto-calendar; warranty obligations tracked |
| Révision des prix | Per CPS formula & index | Flag fixed-price on long durations as risk |
| Paiement | Décomptes provisoires; intérêts moratoires regime on late payment | Feeds VS3 receivables radar |
| Préférence nationale | Up to 15% surcharge applied to foreign bids (works) | Factored into competition analysis |
| Sous-traitance | Within decree limits; declared to buyer | Tracked per project |

## 5. Post-Award (handoff to VS2) and Post-Loss (VS5)

**Won**: notification → caution définitive + insurances (RC chantier, AT,
décennale where applicable) → ordre de service tracking (OS de commencement,
OS d'arrêt/reprise — legal dates matter for penalties) → project record opens in
`project` module with BPU as billing baseline.

**Lost**: capture winner + amount + our gap; request PV extract if useful;
mandatory 5-minute debrief (why lost: price/technical/admin); C1 stores result;
B4 recalibrates win-prob curves; caution provisoire restitution chased to
closure.

**Cancelled/unsuccessful (infructueux)**: recorded — repeated infructueux from a
buyer often precedes a negotiated procedure or relaunch with modified terms:
Sentinel watches for the relaunch.

## 6. Division-Specific Edge Rules

- **Modification of DCE after publication** (avis modificatif/report): Sentinel
  re-diffs documents; Extractor re-parses; Orchestrator re-plans; humans alerted
  if Go already decided.
- **Visite des lieux obligatoire**: missing it is eliminatory — it is a hard
  calendar event with owner + reminder, created at parse time.
- **Échantillons/prototypes**: physical logistics task auto-created with its own
  deadline (often earlier than submission).
- **Groupement opportunities**: when A3 rejects on class/qualification but A4
  scores high, Strategist proposes groupement with mapped partners (from C2
  profiles) — human relationship work follows.
- **Offre anormalement basse**: B4 warns when our scenario crosses the decree's
  abnormally-low thresholds vs estimation — wins that become losses.
